## üìù Tips for Great Recording

1. **Audio Quality**
   - Use a good microphone
   - Record in a quiet room
   - Speak clearly and at moderate pace

2. **Screen Recording**
   - Use OBS Studio (free) or Windows Game Bar (Win + G)
   - Record at 1080p if possible
   - Keep cursor movements smooth

3. **Editing**
   - Cut out long pauses
   - Add background music (optional, low volume)
   - Keep total length under 3 minutes

4. **Before Publishing**
   - Watch it once to catch mistakes
   - Check audio levels (not too loud/quiet)
   - Verify video is clear
